Science Notes

In general relativity, clocks run slower near a black hole because spacetime is strongly curved.

This MGVF-U demonstration keeps that familiar time-dilation story, then adds a visual layer: the vacuum stiffness field. As the ship approaches the black hole, the cockpit instruments show increasing simulated vacuum stiffness.

The time-dilation effect is established physics. The vacuum-stiffness layer is a conceptual MGVF-U visualization, designed for education and storytelling rather than numerical prediction.
